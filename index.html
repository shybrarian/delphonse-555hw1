Homework 1: System Evaluation

I have evaluated the library catalogs of five art museum libraries.

The five art museum libraries are:

  1. The National Gallery of Art Library (NGA; Washington, D.C.)
  2. The Metropolitan Museum of Art Library (Met; New York, NY)
  3. The Getty Museum Library (Getty; Los Angeles, CA)
  4. The John and Mable Ringling Museum of Art Library (Ringling; Sarasota, FL)
  5. The Boston Museum of Fine Arts Library (MFA; Boston, MA)

The three criteria I considered are:

  1. Match between system and the real world
  2. User control and freedom
  3. Flexibility and efficiency of use

These criteria are from Jakob Nielsen's list of 10 Usability Heuristics that we discussed in class.
